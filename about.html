About the Photographer

Based in Kissimmee, Florida, I am a passionate photographer focusing on travel, landscape, and documentary work that tells a story with the technical excellence required for museum-quality prints.

This portfolio houses the best of my travel and landscape photography. Every image here is prepared with my personal artistic vision and technical mastery in mind, with high-quality printing as the primary objective. In the field, I focus on composition, light, and moment to create images that feel distinctive and memorable.

All edits are done exclusively in Lightroom with minimal manipulation—everything you see qualifies for photography competitions where strict standards prohibiting excessive post-processing are upheld.

I'm building a streamlined print store so you can order gallery-quality pieces directly from each collection. In the meantime, feel free to reach out for custom sizes, licensing inquiries, or commission work.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Kissimmee, Florida
  • Focus: Landscape, travel, and documentary photography with contemplative compositions
  • Gear: Fujifilm—travelling light with fast primes and telephoto lenses
  • Philosophy: Let the light, place, and moment speak
